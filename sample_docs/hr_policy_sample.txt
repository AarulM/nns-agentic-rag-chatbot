NEW HIRE ONBOARDING POLICY (SAMPLE / SYNTHETIC DOCUMENT)

1. First Day Checklist
- Report to the Employee Welcome Center by 7:30 AM with government-issued photo ID.
- Complete I-9 verification and W-4 tax forms with HR.
- Receive company badge, PPE starter kit, and parking permit.
- Attend the 2-hour New Employee Safety Orientation before entering any shop floor area.

2. First Week
- Complete online training modules: Code of Conduct, Workplace Harassment Prevention, Export Control Basics.
- Meet with assigned mentor/buddy for a facility tour.
- Enroll in benefits (medical, dental, vision, 401k) within 30 days of start date via the Benefits Portal.
- Set up direct deposit with Payroll.

3. Interns and Co-ops
- Interns must complete the Intern Orientation in addition to standard new hire orientation.
- Interns are issued temporary badges valid only for the duration of their program dates.
- Timesheets must be submitted weekly and approved by the assigned supervisor by end of day Friday.

4. Paid Time Off (PTO)
- Full-time employees accrue 15 days PTO per year in year one, increasing with tenure.
- PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance through the HR portal.
- Sick leave is separate from PTO and does not require advance notice.

5. Who to Contact
- General HR questions: hr-helpdesk@example.com
- Benefits enrollment issues: benefits@example.com
- Badge/access issues: security-badging@example.com
